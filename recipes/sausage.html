Chinese-Style Sausage

Description

This is my favorite recipe from Gregory Gourdet's "Everyone's Table," probably because it's the easiest. Gourdet's recipe calls for ground dark meat chicken, but I've been using ground turkey with great results. These fun, sweet and savory sausage patties are great for breakfast or filling out any grab and go meal. In his book, Gourdet pairs them with eggs and sauteed greens.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs ground turkey
  • 2 tsp kosher salt
  • 3 tbsp coconut aminos or substitute with terriyaki sauce
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp Chinese five spice
  • 3/4 tsp red chile flakes
  • 1/2 tsp white pepper

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients in a mixing bowl and use a spoon to mix well.
  2. Continue mixing until the mixture is of one color and a smooth, sticky consistency.
