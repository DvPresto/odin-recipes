Instant Pot Tuscan Chicken Stew

from Real Food Whole Life

Description

It's finally stew season, so it's tme to get the Instant Pot in full rotation. Full Disclosure: I am an Instant Pot evangelist, and I think Robyn's recipes on realfoodwholelife.com are some of the easiest and tastiest ways to make great use out of the gadget.

This chicken stew is a straight-forward recipe perfect for dinner on those nights when you start to feel a chill in the air. It also a nice way to get an Italian flavor profile into a gluten-free meal plan.

Ingredients

  • 1lb boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup chopped carrots
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1 cup chopped yellow potato
  • 1 15-oz can diced fire-roasted tomato
  • 1 6-oz can tomato paste
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp granulated garlic
  • 32-oz chicken or vegetable stock
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Set the Instant Pot to saute and heat 1 tbsp of olive oil.
  2. Add carrots, celery, and onion and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
